Welcome To The Saylani Welfare

Pakistan

The largest NGO offering free asistant to disaster. Saylani Welfare is on the ground and already working with local communties to access how best to support families in more than 63 million areas of day to day live.

What We Are Doing

Food

Saylani Welfare serves free, nutritious meals daily to thousands of needy people across Pakistan.

Medical

Saylani Medical provides comprehensive free healthcare services across Pakistan—including diagnostics, lab tests (such as cancer screening and blood bank).

Education

Saylani Education provides free quality education and vocational training across Pakistan—operating over 160 schools in underserved areas and offering programs in IT.

Water

Saylani Water (a program under Saylani Welfare Trust) installs free RO filtration plants and provides clean drinking water to underserved communities across Pakistan.
